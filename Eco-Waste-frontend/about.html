About Us

We are dedicated to building a cleaner, greener future by promoting responsible e-waste disposal, recycling, and community education. Our mission is to reduce electronic waste and create a sustainable cycle for the technology we use every day.

Smart E-Waste Collection

We provide safe and easy e-waste collection services for households, schools, and offices. Drop your old devices at our authorized centers or schedule a pickup. Our goal is to ensure electronics are recycled responsibly instead of being dumped into landfills.
